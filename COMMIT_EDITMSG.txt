feat: Add monthly and annual reporting capabilities

This commit introduces monthly and annual reporting functionalities:

- **Monthly/Annual Report DTO:**
  - Created `MonthlyAnnualReportDTO` to represent aggregated data for monthly and annual reports.

- **ReportService Enhancements:**
  - Added `getMonthlyReports` method to generate reports grouped by month for a given year.
  - Added `getAnnualReports` method to generate reports grouped by year for a specified range.

- **ReportController Enhancements:**
  - Exposed new REST endpoints (`/api/reports/monthly` and `/api/reports/annual`) for accessing these reports.

- **Documentation:**
  - Updated `README.md` to reflect the completion of monthly and annual reporting features.
